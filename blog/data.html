What is Datafication?

Data fiction is a current technical phenomenon that aims to convert most aspects of a business into a worthy amount of data that can be tracked, monitored and analyzed. This refers to the use of tools and processes to convert an organization into a data -driven enterprise.

The term "Data Fiction" was introduced by Kenneth Cocherry and Victor Mayor Sachinberg in 2013 to refer to the hidden process converting to data that companies can use to improve their business.
